Walk me through how AI fits into your creative process. Where and when do you find yourself reaching for AI tools?

User: So, I've been using AI recently across all stages in various capacities so I'll go through each area with examples. First up, during the first drafting stage I've been using AI to help with research as well as finding similar works that meet the tone, genre, or overall ideas of the story I'm working on. This has included exploring the main key touchstones of a genre (e.g. What are some key components of a Survival Horror story?, What are some successful Survival Horror books, Films, TV shows and music that evokes the atmosphere). Using this I can develop some inspiration as well as using the music while writing to set the tone. 

During the drafting stage, I use AI to have a quick check of chapters as I write them (or sections depending on the word count) and ask for general feedback. I am careful to specify I don't want suggestions for the story, as I feel this could influence my writing, instead I ask for criticism on the overall tone  and feel. I use the previous conversation  to continue this so the model's know my aim (e.g. Survival Horror Genre) so it can provide relevant feedback. During this process, I sometimes ask the model to lay out the story so far into a story-template such as 'Save The Cat' or 'Three Act Structure' which can help provide a skeleton look at the story to help me navigate and see where the story is sitting in terms of plot. 

Once the first draft is completed. I often ask the AI to provide full feedback on the story and also ask questions such as 'What would a reader feel is missing? Are there any plot points that do not feel fully resolved?' to help me prepare for the 'story draft' I mentioned earlier. 

I usually work through the story draft without using AI too much, as this is the stage where I'm trying to tidy it up so less involvement makes this easier. 

For the Grammar Draft, I usually go through the draft with a checker such as Grammarly, and then once its all done I'll use another AI model to provide feedback and catch anything that may have been missed. This is about the end of the AI use for the story, as I'll turn to human proof readers for the final stages.  

However, once release, I have used AI to help create social media content, but this is an area I'm still quite new at using it for.
