User: Sure. I was grappling with POV in the early stages of my novel. I was writing in first-person POV but events were happening around the country (riots, looting, society breaking down). It did also suggest I gave it a section of my text for it to re-write in a more immersive way, but as already mentioned, that's not something I do.

AI: That's a perfect example of how your reporter protagonist opens up those storytelling possibilities! And I appreciate how you stayed true to your boundary even when AI offered to rewrite text directly.

AI: That's a really thoughtful distinction you're making - between technical competence and those deeper, more nuanced human elements that resonate emotionally with readers. The fact that writing contests are having to explicitly ban AI submissions really speaks to how widespread this has become.
