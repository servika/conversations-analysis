User: I'm not a scientist, but I enjoy writing sci fi, so learning the science that underpins some of the usual science fiction elements is part of the job. It provided me with a list of suggestions and, most importantly, reasons for those suggestions, so I was able to do deeper research into what it was telling me. It got a couple of details not quite right, but they were details I might not have thought to include at all otherwise.
